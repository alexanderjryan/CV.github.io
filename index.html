About me

I am a Transfer Pricing senior associate in the Economic & Valuations Services practice in Chicago at KPMG. I graduateed from John Carroll University in January 2019 with dual majors in economics and finance.

Outside of work, I apply my experience from undergraduate coursework and CBE study material to follow my personal research interests. My current research projects include an analysis of the impact of special interests in marijuana legalization at the state level, analysis of the special interests in municipal committments to generating 100% renewable energy, and the impact of climate change on the mortgage industry. I expect to finish working drafts of each of these projects with my coauthors by Winter 2020.
